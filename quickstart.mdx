## Welcome to CommVoice AI

This guide will help you set up your first AI calling campaign. You'll learn how to configure your AI agent, upload your prospect list, and launch automated calling to start generating qualified leads and sales conversations.

## Step 1: Configure Your AI Agent

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="Create your AI agent persona">
    1. Log in to your CommVoice account at [app.commvoice.ai](https://app.commvoice.ai)
    2. Navigate to "AI Agents" and click "Create New Agent"
    3. Define your agent's personality, voice, and conversation style
    4. Set the agent's name, company role, and background knowledge
    
    <Tip>Choose a personality that matches your brand and target audience for best results.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="script-text" title="Configure conversation scripts">
    1. Set your opening script and introduction
    2. Define key talking points and value propositions
    3. Configure objection handling responses
    4. Set up qualification questions and criteria
    
    <Note>Start with our proven templates and customize them for your specific use case.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 2: Set Up Your Campaign

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Upload your prospect list">
    **Prepare Your Data**:
    - Phone numbers (required)
    - Names and company information
    - Any relevant context or notes
    - Best time to call preferences
    
    **Upload Methods**:
    - CSV file upload (most common)
    - CRM integration sync
    - API integration for real-time data
    - Manual entry for small lists
    
    <Tip>Include as much context as possible to help the AI personalize conversations.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="target" title="Define campaign objectives">
    1. Choose your campaign goal (lead qualification, appointment setting, sales, etc.)
    2. Set qualification criteria and success metrics
    3. Configure follow-up actions for different outcomes
    4. Set calling schedules and time zone preferences
    
    **Common Campaign Types**:
    - **Lead Qualification**: Identify prospects ready to buy
    - **Appointment Setting**: Schedule demos or sales calls
    - **Customer Surveys**: Gather feedback and insights
    - **Event Invitations**: Invite prospects to webinars or events
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 3: Configure Call Outcomes

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="brain" title="Set up AI classification">
  1. Define the outcomes you want to track (Qualified Lead, Not Interested, Callback, etc.)
  2. Configure the AI prompt to identify these outcomes during conversations
  3. Set up automatic actions for each outcome type
  4. Test the classification on sample conversations
  
  **Standard Outcomes**:
  - **Qualified Lead**: Prospect meets your buying criteria
  - **Appointment Scheduled**: Meeting or demo booked
  - **Not Interested**: Prospect declined or not a fit
  - **Callback Requested**: Prospect wants follow-up contact
  - **Voicemail**: Left message on voicemail system
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="arrow-path" title="Configure follow-up actions">
  1. Set up CRM integration to automatically log qualified leads
  2. Configure email notifications for high-priority outcomes
  3. Set up calendar integration for automatic meeting scheduling
  4. Define handoff procedures to your sales team
  
  <Tip>Automate as much as possible to ensure no qualified leads fall through the cracks.</Tip>
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 4: Launch and Monitor

<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Start your AI calling campaign">
  1. Review all campaign settings and AI configuration
  2. Start with a small test batch (10-20 calls) to validate setup
  3. Monitor initial results and adjust if needed
  4. Scale up to full campaign volume once satisfied
  5. Watch real-time results in the dashboard
  
  <Warning>Always test with a small batch first to ensure your AI agent is performing as expected.</Warning>
</Accordion>

## Understanding Your Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="chart-bar" title="Monitor campaign performance">
    **Key Metrics to Watch**:
    - **Connection Rate**: How many calls successfully reach a person
    - **Qualification Rate**: Percentage of connected calls that result in qualified leads
    - **Conversation Quality**: How well the AI is engaging prospects
    - **Outcome Distribution**: Breakdown of call results by category
    
    <Tip>Check your dashboard regularly during the first few hours to catch any issues early.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="ear" title="Review conversation quality">
    1. Listen to sample call recordings to assess AI performance
    2. Read conversation transcripts for quality and accuracy
    3. Check if the AI is following your scripts and handling objections well
    4. Identify opportunities to improve conversation flow
    
    **Quality Indicators**:
    - Natural conversation flow
    - Appropriate responses to objections
    - Successful qualification of prospects
    - Professional and on-brand communication
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Optimization Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Improve AI Performance">
    **Script Optimization**:
    - Refine opening lines based on connection rates
    - Adjust qualification questions for better results
    - Update objection handling based on common responses
    
    **Timing Optimization**:
    - Analyze best calling times for your target audience
    - Adjust calling schedules based on connection patterns
    - Consider time zones and business hours
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Scale Your Success">
    **List Expansion**:
    - Add more prospects to successful campaigns
    - Segment lists for more targeted messaging
    - Use lookalike audiences based on qualified leads
    
    **Campaign Replication**:
    - Clone successful campaigns for new markets
    - A/B test different AI personalities or scripts
    - Expand to additional products or services
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
