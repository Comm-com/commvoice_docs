## AI Campaign Dashboard Interface

The CommVoice dashboard is your central command center for monitoring AI calling campaigns and analyzing performance. It provides real-time insights into your AI agents' activities, conversation outcomes, and campaign effectiveness with intuitive visualizations and key performance indicators.

<Note>
The dashboard updates automatically as your AI agents complete calls. Data typically appears within 30 seconds of call completion.
</Note>

## Dashboard Layout

### Campaign Controls

The dashboard header contains essential campaign management and monitoring controls:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Campaign Selector">

## Key Performance Metrics

The dashboard displays essential AI calling campaign metrics in an organized grid layout:

### Campaign Volume Metrics

<Columns cols={3}>
  <div>
    **Calls Attempted**
    - Total number of calls initiated by AI agents
    - Includes successful connections and failed attempts
    - Real-time counter with hourly breakdown
    - Progress toward daily/campaign goals
  </div>
  <div>
    **Connection Rate**
    - Percentage of calls that successfully connect to a person
    - Excludes voicemail, busy signals, and no-answers
    - Key indicator of list quality and calling time optimization
    - Benchmark against industry standards
  </div>
  <div>
    **Conversation Rate**
    - Percentage of connected calls that result in meaningful conversations
    - Measures AI agent's ability to engage prospects
    - Indicates script effectiveness and personality fit
    - Quality metric beyond basic connection success
  </div>
</Columns>

### AI Performance Metrics

<Columns cols={2}>
  <div>
    **Qualification Success**
    - Percentage of conversations resulting in qualified leads
    - Measures AI agent's ability to identify good prospects
    - Directly impacts pipeline generation and ROI
    - Key indicator of campaign targeting accuracy
  </div>
  <div>
    **Appointment Setting Rate**
    - Percentage of qualified conversations that book meetings
    - Measures AI agent's closing and scheduling effectiveness
    - Critical metric for sales pipeline development
    - Includes confirmed appointments and demo bookings
  </div>
</Columns>

### Conversation Quality Metrics

<Columns cols={3}>
  <div>
    **Average Conversation Length**
    - Mean duration of successful prospect conversations
    - Indicates engagement level and conversation depth
    - Longer conversations often correlate with better outcomes
    - Benchmark for AI agent script optimization
  </div>
  <div>
    **Objection Handling Rate**
    - Percentage of objections successfully addressed by AI
    - Measures AI agent's ability to overcome resistance
    - Key indicator of script quality and training effectiveness
    - Critical for improving conversion rates
  </div>
  <div>
    **Positive Sentiment Score**
    - Average sentiment rating of prospect interactions
    - Measures prospect satisfaction with AI conversations
    - Important for brand reputation and future opportunities
    - Tracked through conversation analysis
  </div>
</Columns>

## Troubleshooting and Support

### Common Issues and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Performance Issues">
    **Low Connection Rates**:
    - Check calling times and time zone settings
    - Verify phone number quality and DNC compliance
    - Analyze list sources and data freshness
    - Consider caller ID and reputation factors
    
    **Poor Conversation Quality**:
    - Review AI agent script adherence
    - Analyze objection handling effectiveness
    - Check conversation flow and pacing
    - Optimize opening scripts and value propositions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Technical Issues">
    **Dashboard Loading Problems**:
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Check internet connection stability
    - Try different browser or incognito mode
    - Contact support for persistent issues
    
    **Data Sync Issues**:
    - Verify CRM integration status
    - Check API connection health
    - Review data mapping and field configurations
    - Monitor integration error logs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
