## What are AI Agents?

AI Agents in CommVoice are intelligent virtual representatives that make phone calls on behalf of your business. Each AI agent has a unique personality, conversation style, and specialized knowledge that allows them to conduct natural, effective conversations with your prospects and customers.

Your AI agents are equipped with advanced conversation capabilities:

  <Tab title="Emotional Intelligence">

    **Conflict Resolution**: Handles objections and concerns with patience and professionalism

  <Tab title="Business Intelligence">

    **Product Expertise**: Deep understanding of your products, services, and value propositions
